Welcome to our tutorial section!
These learning-oriented guides would help you get started
and build your skills progressively.

## What are tutorials? {/* #what-are-tutorials */}

Tutorials are **learning-oriented** lessons
that guide you through a series of steps to complete a meaningful project
or achieve a learning goal.
They assume no prior knowledge and focus on helping you learn by doing.

## Tutorial characteristics {/* #tutorial-characteristics */}

- **Learning-focused**: Designed to teach, not complete tasks
- **Beginner-friendly**: Assume no prior knowledge
- **Step-by-step**: Clear, sequential instructions
- **Practical**: Learn by building something real
- **Guided**: We tell you exactly what to do
