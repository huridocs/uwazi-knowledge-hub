This section contains **problem-oriented** guides
that help you do specific tasks or solve particular problems.

## What are how-to guides? {/* #what-are-how-to-guides */}

How-to guides are practical instructions focused on solving real-world problems.
They assume some familiarity with the product and get straight to the solution.

## Guide characteristics {/* #guide-characteristics */}

- **Goal-oriented**: Focused on achieving specific outcomes
- **Practical**: Real-world scenarios and solutions
- **Efficient**: Direct path to the solution
- **Assume knowledge**: Basic familiarity with the product
