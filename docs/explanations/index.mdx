This section provides **understanding-oriented** content that explains concepts, design decisions, and architectural principles.

## What are Explanations? {/* #what-are-explanations */}

Explanations help you understand the product's design, the reasoning behind decisions, and how different parts work together. They provide context and deepen your knowledge.

## Explanation Characteristics {/* #explanation-characteristics */}

- **Understanding-focused**: Explains the "why" and "how"
- **Contextual**: Provides background and reasoning
- **Conceptual**: Covers ideas and principles
- **Discursive**: Written in a flowing, narrative style
